Build a Table of Contents

By hand.

Building a table of contents by hand may seem like the easiest approach, but if your content is still in flux, or is likely to change, then you're better off using an automated solution to generate the list.

Automate it.

My friend Sanders Kleinfeld at O'Reilly wrote some XSL to automatically pull together a table of contents from your section headings. The resulting table of contents will be formatted as an ordered list within a nav element, and requires that your HTML follows the HTMLBook specification.
